Community Engagement and CSR Activities

At AAD Hospital, we believe in giving back to the community. Our commitment to social responsibility extends beyond medical services. We actively engage in various community outreach programs and support initiatives that make a positive impact on society.

Free Health Camps

Our free health camps provide essential healthcare services to underprivileged communities. We conduct regular check-ups, screenings, and health consultations, ensuring that quality healthcare reaches everyone.

Learn More

Blood Donation Drives

In partnership with local organizations, AAD Hospital organizes frequent blood donation drives to support the community’s blood bank needs. Join us in saving lives through regular blood donations.

Learn More

Environmental Initiatives

Our hospital is committed to sustainable practices. We actively participate in tree plantation drives and promote eco-friendly practices, reducing our environmental footprint.

Learn More

Health Awareness Programs

To improve public health, we conduct health awareness programs on various topics like diabetes, hypertension, mental health, and nutrition. We aim to educate and empower the community to lead healthier lives.
