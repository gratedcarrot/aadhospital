About Us

AAD Multispeciality hospitals were started by three new generation enterpreniours - Aryan Paode, Akshay Sathaye and Dhruv Dhilla in 2022.

It staretd with initial 500 bed hospital in Mumbai and soon expanded to Delhi, Pune and Bangalore. Each of these hospitals are equipped with world class diagnostic and treatment facilties and renowned full time and visiting doctors. Our Mumbai and Delhi hospitals are accredited with honour of being amongst top 10 multi-speciality hospitals in India by Medial Association of India.

Our hospitals cater to all segments of society and also support econimically weaker section with completely free of cost services under Ayushman Bharat Scheme.

We aim to open 20 new fully equipped multispeciality hospitals in tier-3 cities soon with an objective to provide world class medical facilities even in smaller towns.
