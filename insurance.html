TPA Partners, Cashless Insurance Services, and Crowdfunding Options

Our hospital collaborates with multiple TPA (Third Party Administrators) and insurance providers to offer seamless cashless services for our patients. We are committed to making healthcare accessible through transparent insurance processes and by supporting crowdfunding initiatives.

Our TPA Partners

We work with the following TPA partners to facilitate cashless healthcare services:

  • Medicare TPA Services
  • Paramount Health Services
  • Family Health Plan TPA Ltd.
  • Raksha TPA
  • HealthIndia Insurance TPA

Insurance Plans and Process

We accept a wide range of health insurance plans to cover your medical expenses. Here's the process for availing insurance:

  1. Verify your insurance policy coverage with our team.
  2. Submit the necessary documents, including ID proof, policy details, and medical history.
  3. Our team will coordinate with the TPA and insurance provider for approval.
  4. Once approved, cashless services will be available for your treatment.
View Accepted Insurance Plans

How to Avail Cashless Services

Our hospital offers cashless services for patients insured under our partner insurance providers. To avail cashless services, follow these steps:

  • Contact our billing desk for assistance with cashless procedures.
  • Provide valid insurance and TPA details before admission.
  • Our team will handle the claim process, allowing you to focus on your recovery.

Support through Crowdfunding

For patients facing financial difficulties, we provide the option to initiate crowdfunding to cover their treatment costs. We collaborate with trusted crowdfunding platforms to ensure transparency and safety for our patients.
